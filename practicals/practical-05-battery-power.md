## Part B — No-power / no-charge diagnosis stations (70 min)

Three mystery phones, each with ONE planted fault. **Follow the flow IN ORDER for every phone —
no skipping ahead, even if you think you already know.**

**The flow:**
1. **Visual** — port, cable, case, screen, signs of liquid or swelling
2. **Battery voltage** — multimeter on battery terminals, RECORD the reading
3. **Known-good battery swap** — does it power on now?
4. **Ammeter** — known-good cable + charger through the USB ammeter, RECORD the reading
5. **Verdict** — name the fault, fix it if bench-fixable, verify
